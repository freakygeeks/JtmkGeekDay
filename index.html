Lite Netrider - King Of The Hill (KOTH)



KOTH competition is a multi user game created using multi user functionality of Packet Tracer.


The objective of the game is to explore and troubleshoot a routed network to get privileged EXEC access to the Hill Router. Enjoy this competition to test and brush-up your skills in network routing!


This competion is open to every student at JTMK, also it is open book and open Internet. Top three group who manage to get to the top will be giving an exciting prize!


Every student who participate in this competition will be given a certificate of participation.



Partipation is by group. Each group should consist of two person only.


Date : 18th Mac 2016


Time : 2.00 pm - 5.00 pm


Venue : NWL Lab, JTMK

Creative Video



Video Creative competition is a video submission competition based on creativity of students to explain on subtopic of their core subject.


JTMK students who enrolled in DIP program and DNS program should choose one subtopic from these program core subject and create a creative video to explain on that subtopic.


An exciting prizes will be given to the top three best video submitted!


Every student who participate in this competition will be given a certificate of participation.



Partipation is by group. Each group should consist of three person only.


Date Submission : 9th Mac 2016 - 18th Mac 2016


Video Length : 3 minutes - 7 minutes


Video submission : Compact Disk (CD)
